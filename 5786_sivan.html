רִבּוֹן כָּל הַמַּעֲשִׂים וְאַדוֹן כָּל הַנְּשָׁמוֹת

(modeh ani l'fanecha melech (chai)(2x) v'kayam

(v'ahavta l'rei'acha kamocha)(3x)

(v'ahavta l'rei'acha kamocha)(3x)

(atah b'ratah y'tzartah v'nafachtah

I give thanks to You living and everlasting ruler for You have restored my soul with mercy.
Great is Your faithfulness.
So long as the soul is within me I give thanks to You, my God, and God of my ancestors, Lord of all creatures, Master of all souls.
You shall love your neighbor as yourself – that verse represents the central tenet of the Torah & faith.
You created it, formed it, breathed it into me. You preserve it within me. You will eventually take it from me, and restore it in me in the time to come.

9.Nasu (Psalm 93:3-5/Josh Warshawsky)

נָשְׂאוּ נְהָרוֹת יְיָ. נָשְׂאוּ נְהָרוֹת קוֹלָם

מִקֹּלוֹת מַיִם רַבִּים אַדִּירִים מִשְׁבְּרֵי יָם
מִקּלוֹת מַיִם רַבִּים אַדִּיר בַּמָּרוֹם יְיָ
עֵדֹתֶיךָ נֶאֱמְנוּ מְאֹד
לְבֵיתְךָ נַאֲוָה קֹדֶשׂ, יְיָ לְאֹרֶךְ יָמִים

nasu n'harot adonai, nasu n'harot kolam
nasu n'harot adonai, yisu n'harot dochyam
mikolot mayim rabim, adirim mishb'rei yam
mikolot mayim rabim, adir bamarom adonai
eidotecha ne'emnu me'od
l'veitcha na'avah kodesh
adonai l'orech yamim

The ocean sounds, Holy One,
The ocean sounds its thunder,
The ocean sounds its pounding.
And above the thunder of the mighty waters,
more majestic than the breakers of the sea
is the Holy One, majestic on high,
If only you can hear the sound.
Your wisdom and teachings continue to endure;
It’s beautiful and grand, to come into Your holy house,
Holy One, for all times.

15.S'firat ha'omer (Leviticus 23:15-16, Yosef Goldman)

לְשֵׁם יִחוּד קּוּדְשָׁא בְּרִיךְ הוּא וּשְׁכִינְתֵּיהּ
הִנְּנִי מוּכָן וּמְזֻמָּן לְקַיֵּם מִצְוַת עֲשֵׂה שֶׁל סְפִירַת הָעֹמֶר
''שֶׁבַע שַׁבָּתוֹת תְּמִימוֹת תִּהְיֶינָה''
בָּרוּךְ אַתָּה יְיָ אֱלֹהֵינוּ מֶלֶךְ הָעוֹלָם
אֲשֶׁר קִדְּשָׁנוּ בְּמִצְוֹתָיו, וְצִוָּנוּ עַל סְפִירַת הָעֹמֶר

l'sheim yichud kud'shah b'rich hu ushchinteih
hin'ni muchan um'zuman l'kayeim mitzvat aseih
(shel s'firat ha'omer)(4x)
(sheva shabbatot t'mimot tihyenah)(2x)
(shel s'firat ha'omer)(4x)
baruch atah adonai eloheinu melech ha'olam asher kidshanu b'mitzvotav v'tzivanu (al s'firat ha'omer)(4x)

For the sake of the unification of the transcendent creative power with the divine presence dwelling here and now, I am here, I am ready to fulfill the mitzvah of counting the Omer. “...Seven weeks. They must be complete…” Blessed are You, Adonai our God, Ruler of the universe, who sanctifies us with mitzvot, and commands us concerning the counting of the Omer.

הַיּוֹם שִׁשָּׁה וּשְׁלֹשִׁים יוֹם שֶׁהֵם חֲמִשָּׁה שָׁבוּעוֹת וְיוֹם אֶחָד לָעֹֽמֶר

hayom shishah ushloshim yom sheheim chamishah shavu'ot v'yom echad la'omer

Today is thirty-six days, which is five weeks and one day of the Omer.
